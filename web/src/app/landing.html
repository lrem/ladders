Already on a ladder? Custom ranked ladders Ladders is a free service that allows you to track your own custom leagues. It uses the patented Microsoft® TrueSkill™ algorithm to compute a reliable ranking assigning a single skill number to each of the players. It is somewhat similar to Elo, the rating system widely used in chess and similar games, except here you can have games with any number of teams and players. Get started All you need is to know are the rules of the game you are playing... And have an idea for a name of your custom ranked ladder. Create a new ladder!
